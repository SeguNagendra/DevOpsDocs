# AWS Service Categories

AWS offers **hundreds of services**, and that can feel overwhelming at first.

The key to understanding AWS is **not memorizing services**, but understanding **how AWS groups them by purpose**.

Once you get these categories right:
- AWS stops feeling confusing
- Architecting becomes logical
- Interviews become easier

---

## Why Service Categories Matter

Without categories:
- AWS looks like a random list of services
- Beginners feel lost
- Wrong services get chosen

With categories:
- You understand *why* a service exists
- You know *where* it fits
- You choose services confidently

## How DevOps Engineers Use Categories

DevOps engineers:
- Think in categories, not services
- Combine multiple categories per architecture
- Optimize cost, security, and performance together

This mindset separates **engineers from button-clickers**.
